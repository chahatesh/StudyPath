Built to close the gap between studying and actually learning.

Most study tools treat every student the same. StudyPath doesn't. When you start, you take a quick 8-question quiz to find your learning style. From that point on, every explanation, quiz hint, and lesson adapts specifically to you.

Visual learners get diagrams. Auditory learners get prompts to say things aloud. Read/write learners get structured notes. Kinesthetic learners get real-world practice problems. The same content — delivered the way your brain actually absorbs it.

🎯 Personalised from question one

Learning Styles

Which type of learner are you?

StudyPath supports all four VARK learning styles. Take the quiz in the app and every single feature will adapt to you.

👁️

Visual Learner

You think in diagrams, charts, and pictures. StudyPath gives you visual maps and spatial prompts.

🎧

Auditory Learner

You learn by talking and listening. StudyPath prompts you to say concepts aloud and discuss ideas.

✍️

Reading/Writing Learner

You thrive on notes and text. StudyPath gives you structured definitions, lists, and writing prompts.

🤲

Kinesthetic Learner

You learn by doing. StudyPath connects every concept to real-world examples and practice problems.

Just so you know

This is a hobby project. ✌️

StudyPath was built in my spare time as a personal project — not for a grade, not for a class, just because I wanted to build something genuinely useful for students like me. Every question, every feature, and every design decision was made by one person figuring things out as they went.

It's not perfect, and it's always getting better. If you spot a bug, have a suggestion, or just want to say something — I'd love to hear from you.
